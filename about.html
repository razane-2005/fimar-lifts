Fimar Lifts

Fimar Lifts is a trusted elevator company established in 1997. Over the years, the company has grown steadily and built a strong reputation in Lebanon.

We specialize in elevator installation, maintenance, and repair. Our team is capable of building elevators from scratch as well as fixing and upgrading existing systems.

With years of experience, we focus on delivering safe, modern, and reliable lift solutions for homes and buildings.

Founder

The company is proudly managed by Haitham Soueid, who has led Fimar Lifts with dedication, expertise, and a commitment to quality.
